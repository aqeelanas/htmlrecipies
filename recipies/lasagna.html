How to make Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a popular Italian pasta dish known for its delicious layers of wide, flat pasta sheets, rich tomato sauce, creamy béchamel sauce, flavorful ground meat (usually beef or pork), and a generous amount of melted cheese, typically mozzarella and Parmesan. The ingredients are stacked in layers and then baked until the pasta is tender and the cheese on top is golden and bubbling. This hearty and comforting dish is often served in square or rectangular slices and is a favorite in many parts of the world for its savory and satisfying flavors. It's a classic comfort food that brings together the wonderful combination of pasta, sauce, and cheese in every bite.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles: Wide, flat pasta sheets, either dried or fresh.
  • Ground meat: Ground beef, pork, or a combination of both.
  • Onion and garlic: These add flavor to the meat sauce.
  • Tomato sauce: A rich, tomato-based sauce often seasoned with herbs like basil and oregano.

Steps

  1. Heat a bit of olive oil in a large skillet or saucepan over medium heat
  2. Sauté finely chopped onions and minced garlic until translucent.
  3. Drain excess fat if necessary.
